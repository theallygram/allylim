Case Studies

Publishing and QA for Telecom/Regulatory Content at ISED

Role: Editor, Content QA, Web Publisher Audience: Industry professionals, licensees, legal/regulatory stakeholders

Supported the publication of spectrum licensing documents, policy consultations, and technical bulletins for Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada. Edited and quality-checked public-facing materials including the 3800 MHz Spectrum Licence Maps and SAB-001-23 bulletin.

These documents require precise technical language, regulatory compliance, and clear structure for diverse audiences. Helped prepare 2,000+ web pages for a CMS migration, ensuring content integrity and consistent formatting throughout the transition.

Why this work matters

  • Complexity: Telecom licensing content involves dense regulatory language, geographic data, and cross-referenced policy frameworks.
  • Clarity techniques: Consistent heading hierarchy, scannable tables, plain-language summaries for technical audiences.
  • Scale: QA across hundreds of interlinked documents with strict accuracy requirements.
